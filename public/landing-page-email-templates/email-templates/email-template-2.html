The 2020 State of Open Source License Compliance

The idea that open source software is “free” is a misnomer. Free of cost, yes, but not free of obligations and management. Open source licenses are binding contracts that describe what users can legally do with the software components. Essentially it is the license of a software component that makes it open source.
